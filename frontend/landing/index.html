How it works

No setup, no accounts. One host screen, phones as controllers, straight into the lobby.

  1. 1

    Open the host

    Launch the host on a TV or laptop. A room code and QR code pop up instantly.

  2. 2

    Scan with phones

    Everyone scans the QR or types the 4-letter code. Their phone becomes the controller.

  3. 3

    Race or wreck

    Pick a mode and go. Grab weapons, take corners, and be the last car standing.
